I'm a great cook

Hello my dear friends; Today I am going to tell you about the stuffed potato!! Sounds yummy snd it is easy to cook. Here are the ingredients for 3persons; 6 large potatoes, 6 tablespoons butter, 6 slices bacon, shredded cheese, 4 mushrooms, one bell pepper and 1/2 teaspoon salt.

Heat the oven to 375F in the meantime rub the whole potatoes with softened butter. Then, put all potatoes on the oven and bake them for 30 minutes, make sure the potatoes are tender when poking them with a fork. After baking the potatoes, cut them in half and remove inside the potatoes with a teaspoon, leave a tiny shell to hold them tight.

Chop mushrooms and a pepper after washing them. After that put bacons, mushrooms and peppers in the potatoes and add shredded cheese on the top of that. Now it's ready to go to oven and wait 20 minutes to serve the meal.

bon appetit!!
